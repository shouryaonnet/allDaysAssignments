Plot

"The Originals" follows the Mikaelson siblings — the world's first vampires — as they return to the city they helped build, New Orleans. With their power, history, and deep family ties, they face supernatural threats, betrayal, and their own dark legacies.

Centered around Klaus Mikaelson, the story explores themes of loyalty, redemption, and power. As Klaus attempts to reclaim his city from a growing number of enemies, his siblings Elijah and Rebekah struggle to keep their fractured family intact. The show delves into vampire-witch politics, werewolf rivalries, and the mystical balance of power that governs the city. With rich lore and gothic drama, "The Originals" expands the universe of The Vampire Diaries and offers a darker, more mature tone.
